Top Ten Tips for eating well

  1. Eat regularly
  2. Eat at least five portions of fruit and vegetables a day
  3. Cut down on saturated fat
  4. Eat less salt
  5. Swap meat for fish
  6. Keep an eye on portion sizes - quality not quantity!
  7. Swap your snacks for healthier options (e.g., apple instead of a biscuit)
  8. Think about drinks (some drinks have more calories than you think!)
  9. Read food labels - it is an important habit to get into
  10. Choose well when you are eating out - pick from a menu before you go out so you won't be tempted
