Tips to manage stress

  1. If you often feel stressed or anxious, it's important to learn how to relax. This can allow you to take a step back from the stress Click on one of the following options to try one our guided relaxation (Script developed by KUL)
    • visualisation
    • deep breathing
  2. Physical activity, eating well and getting enough sleep and rest can all help you to manage your stress.
