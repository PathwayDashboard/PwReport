Being active helps keep your heart healthy.

You can plan how you want to be active. This is your personal goal and you can change it however you would like, once you reach your weekly target

There are several reasons why being active helps keep your heart healthy:

  • It gives you heart some needed exercise and keeps it healthy and strong
  • Helps lower your cholesterol
  • Helps lower your blood pressure
  • It can also help you manage stress
  • It can give you a mood boost
  • It can increase you energy levels-especially if you are moving toward you goals!
