Drinking more than the recommended amount of alcohol damage your heart health

It can cause abnormal heart rhythms, high blood pressure, and can damage the heart muscle itself.

Alcohol also has limited nutritional benefit but can be high in calories. This can lead to weight gain.

Alcohol can also lower your will power. This can make it much easier to to give into food cravings that you could otherwise avoid.

What are the recommendations?

To stay healthy:
  • Women should not regularly drink more than 2 or 3 units of alcohol a day
  • Men should not regularly drink more than 3 or 4 units of alcohol a day.

Understanding units:

The number of units in a drink is worked out using the size and strength of the drink. Different drinks will have different units e.g., a glass of wine will be more units than a glass of beer of the same size.
